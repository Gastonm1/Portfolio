My Story

Prior to pivoting my career towards software development, I worked at a law firm in the Chicago metropolitan area. My ability to quickly learn and meticulous attention to detail helped me excel at the firm but I longed for something that would allow me to express my creative side and utilize love to solve problems.

After some introductory online classes (codeacaedemy, udemy), I knew software engineering was the career path for me. In September 2018, I enrolled in Northwestern University's intensive Fullstack coding program. Here, I have acquired the skills to become proficient in Fullstack development. I anticipate graduation from the Fullstack Development program in May 2019. While, I am confident in my newfound skill set, education and teaching experiences from Northwestern University, I am looking for an opportunity to grow as a developer while utilizing my professional experience of working in a team environment.

I have experience in implementation and testing for small to large scale applications including sustaining small and large scale applications with constant updates. I strive to create the best experience for the customer in the form of usable and reliable software. My specialties include frontend integration along with data manipulation.
